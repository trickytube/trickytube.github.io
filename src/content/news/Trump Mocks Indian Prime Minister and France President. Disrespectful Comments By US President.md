Recent comments by Donald Trump have once again created tension in global diplomacy. His statements targeting leaders of friendly nations such as India and France have raised questions about America’s diplomatic approach during his leadership.

### Trump Mockery of India's PM Narendra Modi
Trump publicly made remarks about Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, portraying him in a submissive tone through a story that many believe was exaggerated or untrue. speaking at House GOP MEMBER RETREAT, Trump said that Indian Prime Minister Modi came to see me . **Sir  may i see you , Please? yes" he said ,answering him** . Such comments were seen as unnecessary and disrespectful, especially between two countries that share strong strategic and economic ties. Following  are the different context for which Trump recounted a Conversation with PM Modi in a weird way.
#### Slow Delivery of Apache helicopters
The issue highlighted by Trump was the delayed delivery of Apache attack helicopters ordered by India from the United States. He expressed frustration over the slow pace, claiming that some deliveries had been pending for several years. However, defense deals often face delays due to paperwork, approvals, and production schedules, making public criticism of a partner country diplomatically awkward.
#### India's continued purchase of Russian Oil
Trump also voiced strong criticism over India’s continued purchase of Russian crude oil. Despite warnings from US and possible trade penalties, India has chosen to protect its energy needs and economic stability. This decision reflects India’s independent foreign policy, where national interest takes priority over external pressure.

### Decline Of Trump's approval rating
According to political observers, Trump’s sharp and confrontational tone may also be linked to his domestic political challenges. During this period, his approval ratings had declined , Firstly his approval rating was fallen to 39% and slightly recovered to 42% due to his entertaining online contents. Many of his public statements appeared aimed at appealing to his political base rather than maintaining calm international relations.
### Trump Mockery of France's President Emmanuel macron
Not only India but also France was  drawn into the controversy. French President Emmanuel Macron criticized US actions related to regime change efforts, warning that such moves weaken international law. Trump responded by mocking Macron personally, including his accent and the way he addressed him as Trump but not as "Mr. President", further straining diplomatic ties.

### Conclusion
Overall, these episodes show the difficulties faced by countries dealing with an unpredictable US leadership style. For India, the challenge lies in carefully managing relations with America maintaining strong trade and defense cooperation while firmly protecting its own strategic and economic interests. In uncertain political times, diplomacy often becomes a test of patience and long-term planning rather than quick reactions.
